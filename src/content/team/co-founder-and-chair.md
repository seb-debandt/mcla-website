bio: >-
  Sebastien de Bandt is co-founder and Chair of the MCLA. In 2022, at the age of
  37, he was diagnosed with mantle cell lymphoma and underwent treatment
  following the Nordic protocol. He has been in remission ever since.

  A self-described “techno optimist,” Sebastien believes that as scientific research accelerates, a future where all diseases are manageable is within reach. Through MCLA, he is committed to helping patients and families today—while supporting the momentum to speed up progress in research and access to better care.


  Sebastien has spent most of his career in the tech industry, both as a startup founder and in senior leadership roles within established digital businesses, bringing a mix of entrepreneurial energy and operational experience to the MCLA’s mission.
