bio: "Linda Trummer is a community builder, cancer‑advocate, and survivor whose
  work is rooted in compassion, resilience, and decades of service to others.
  After her own diagnosis with Mantle Cell Lymphoma, she founded Mantle Cell
  Lymphoma: The Journey, a global support community of 2,600+ patients and
  caregivers across 40 countries, offering trauma‑informed guidance and a sense
  of belonging to people facing a rare and difficult disease. Her leadership is
  shaped by 25 years directing the Meadowbrook Collaborative in St. Louis Park,
  MN, where she strengthened health, education, and safety for a high-risk
  population. A longtime civic leader, Linda has been recognized with multiple
  local and national honors, including awards from the Minnesota Chiefs of
  Police and special recognition from the White House. She carries the
  understanding that what we’ve had to learn the hard way can become a soft
  place for someone else to land."
