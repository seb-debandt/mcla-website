  Dave is an attorney at Microsoft, where he serves as Assistant General Counsel
  on the Product Compliance Team. He has held various roles in Microsoft's legal
  department for almost fourteen years. In addition to his legal experience,
  Dave brings valuable board service, having previously served on the board of
  Washington Lawyers for the Arts.

  Dave is deeply committed to the mission of the MCLA. In 2024, at age 44, he was diagnosed with Mantle Cell Lymphoma and initially faced challenges finding up-to-date information and a patient community. Now in remission, he is dedicated to helping build an organization that serves the global MCL community, advocates for patients, and advances the search for a cure.
