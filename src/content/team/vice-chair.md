bio: >-
  Lawrence Fagan, MD, PhD was diagnosed with MCL in 2012. He is the moderator of
  the ACOR MCL support group and participates in six additional online MCL
  communities. Larry helped create a list of key MCL websites and coordinates
  zoom presentations by MCL experts for the online patient community. He
  represents patient interests in projects at Sutter Health (Northern
  California), University of Colorado, and

  City of Hope. Prior to diagnosis, Larry was a Senior Research Scientist at Stanford University focused

  at the intersection of computers and medicine on research projects in oncology, HIV/AIDS, and critical care medicine. He is a Fellow of the American College of Medical Informatics and a Fellow of the American Medical Informatics Association.
