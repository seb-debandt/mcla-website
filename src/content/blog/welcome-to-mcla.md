We are proud to announce the launch of the **Mantle Cell Lymphoma Alliance (MCLA)**, a new U.S. nonprofit dedicated to supporting those affected by mantle cell lymphoma.

## Why MCLA?

Mantle cell lymphoma is a rare and often aggressive form of non-Hodgkin lymphoma. While treatment options have expanded in recent years, patients and caregivers still face significant challenges in accessing clear information, finding experienced specialists, and connecting with others on a similar journey.

MCLA was founded to address these gaps. We believe that by bringing together patients, caregivers, researchers, and clinicians, we can create a stronger, more informed community — one that drives better outcomes and accelerates progress toward a cure.

## Our Mission

The Mantle Cell Lymphoma Alliance supports those facing mantle cell lymphoma with trusted information and expert-guided support. We connect patients, caregivers, researchers, and oncologists across all clinical settings to improve outcomes and expand access to care.

## What's Ahead

In the coming months, we will be expanding our resources, launching educational programs, and building partnerships with leading medical institutions. We invite you to join us on this journey.

Whether you are a patient, caregiver, researcher, or clinician — **you are welcome here**.

Stay connected with us by subscribing to our newsletter and following us on social media.
