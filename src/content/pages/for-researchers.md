MCLA is committed to supporting and accelerating research that improves outcomes for MCL patients. We welcome partnerships with researchers and institutions who share our commitment to advancing MCL science.

## How We Support Research

- **Funding:** As MCLA grows, we plan to offer research grants focused on our priority areas. We are currently building our grants program and welcome expressions of interest.
- **Patient engagement:** We can help connect researchers with MCL patients for studies, surveys, and clinical trial recruitment.
- **Education and dissemination:** We translate research findings for patient audiences and help increase awareness of important studies and clinical trial opportunities.
- **Advocacy:** We advocate for increased federal and private research funding for MCL and for policies that support clinical trial access.

## Research Partnership Opportunities

We are interested in partnerships that:

- Address our stated research priorities
- Include patient-reported outcomes and quality of life measures
- Advance understanding of MCL biology and treatment resistance
- Expand access to clinical trials for underserved populations
- Develop educational materials based on the latest evidence

## Clinical Trial Support

We believe clinical trials are essential for advancing MCL treatment. MCLA can help with trial awareness and patient education, connecting patients with trial opportunities, developing patient-friendly trial information, and advocating for trial design that prioritizes patient experience.
