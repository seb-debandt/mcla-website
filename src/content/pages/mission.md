missionStatement: The Mantle Cell Lymphoma Alliance supports everyone facing
  mantle cell lymphoma with trusted information and expert‑guided support. We
  put research at the center of the fight against mantle cell lymphoma, raising
  funds to drive better treatments and, ultimately, a cure. By supporting
  promising research and making complex science understandable, we help
  patients, caregivers, researchers, and oncologists work together across all
  clinical settings to improve outcomes and support informed care wherever
  people are treated.
vision: We envision a future in which every person affected by mantle cell
  lymphoma has access to expert‑informed care, clear treatment choices, and a
  strong support community — and where sustained, collaborative research leads
  to better therapies, longer lives, and, ultimately, a cure.
