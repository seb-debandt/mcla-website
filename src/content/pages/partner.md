MCLA believes that meaningful progress against MCL requires collaboration. We welcome partnerships with medical institutions, research organizations, pharmaceutical companies, and other nonprofits who share our commitment to improving outcomes for MCL patients.

## Partnership Opportunities

### Medical & Research Institutions

- Collaborate on patient education programs
- Partner on research initiatives and clinical trial awareness
- Contribute medical expertise to our content review process
- Co-host educational events and webinars

### Industry Partners

- Support educational programs through grants
- Sponsor awareness campaigns and events
- Collaborate on clinical trial education and access initiatives
- Provide unrestricted educational grants

### Nonprofit & Community Organizations

- Co-develop patient support programs
- Share resources and best practices
- Cross-promote events and initiatives
- Collaborate on advocacy efforts

## Our Partnership Principles

All MCLA partnerships are guided by:

- **Editorial independence:** MCLA maintains full editorial control over all content and programs.
- **Transparency:** We publicly disclose all partnerships and funding sources.
- **Patient focus:** Every partnership must ultimately benefit MCL patients and caregivers.
- **Evidence-based:** All supported programs and content are grounded in current medical evidence.
