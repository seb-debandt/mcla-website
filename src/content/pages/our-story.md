The Mantle Cell Lymphoma Alliance was born out of a deeply personal experience. When our founders first encountered an MCL diagnosis in their family, they found themselves navigating a landscape with limited resources specifically tailored to this rare cancer.

General cancer resources existed in abundance, but finding answers to the specific questions that MCL patients and caregivers face — about treatment approaches, specialist expertise, research progress, and long-term management — was far more challenging.

> "We realized that if we — with access to good healthcare and a strong support network — struggled to find clear, reliable MCL-specific information, the challenge must be even greater for others."

That realization became the catalyst for MCLA. We set out to build something that didn't yet exist: a dedicated organization that combines trusted medical information, direct connections to MCL expertise, support for targeted research, and a sense of community for everyone touched by this disease.

## Where We're Headed

We are in the early stages of building MCLA, but our ambitions are clear. We are developing partnerships with leading medical institutions, creating educational programs for patients and caregivers, establishing a research grants program, and building a platform where the MCL community can connect and support one another.

We believe that progress against MCL requires collaboration — between patients and their care teams, between researchers across institutions, and between those facing this disease and those working to advance treatments. MCLA exists to make those connections happen.
